Information We Collect

Vocal Studio is designed to protect your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best vocal training experience:

  • Your preferred name, email, and password for authentication
  • Voice related data to improve performance and user experience

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the Vocal Studio app functionality
  • Store your vocal exercises and progress locally on your device
  • Provide technical support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Your voice exercise collections and practice data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload on external servers. This ensures your vocal practice remains private and secure.

Third-Party Services

Vocal Studio may use third-party services for analytics and app performance monitoring. These services operate under their own privacy policies and do not have access to your audio recordings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your vocal recordings remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
