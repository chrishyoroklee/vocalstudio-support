Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Open the app and create a collection and choose an exercise! Feel free to explore our entire exercise library.

How do I change keys?

You can change the pitch range by clicking on the circular button with note name like D4. Top button is the starting pitch and bottom button is the ending pitch.

Can I access Vocal Studio without wifi?

The data is stored locally on your phone, so you can practice without needing wifi.
